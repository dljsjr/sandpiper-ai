# Sandpiper Mascot Design Concept

Based on the non-breeding adult sandpiper reference image, here's a concept for a highly detailed sandpiper mascot with depth and shading, similar to the Pi mascot or Copilot logo examples.

## Design Approach

The mascot should use:
- Extensive block characters (█, ▓, ▒, ▀, ▄, ▌, ▐, etc.) for depth and texture
- Multiple theme colors for shading and detail
- Careful arrangement to create 3D appearance
- Profile view with characteristic sandpiper posture

## Conceptual Structure (6-8 lines):

Line 1: Head and eye detail with proper shading
Line 2: Beak and neck transition
Line 3: Body with wing detail
Line 4: Lower body and leg attachment
Line 5: Legs with proper proportion
Line 6: Feet and ground interaction
Line 7: Ground texture/base (optional)
Line 8: Additional detail/shadow (optional)

## Color Strategy

Similar to the Pi mascot example:
- Primary color for main body
- Secondary color for shadows/highlights
- Accent color for eye/detail
- Dim color for contrast/depth

## Reference Inspiration

The design should capture:
- The sandpiper's distinctive long beak
- The bent-over feeding posture
- The slender legs and small feet
- The compact body shape
- The overall "busy" texture of feathers

The mascot should be recognizable as a sandpiper while using the sophisticated coloring and block character techniques seen in professional mascot designs.
